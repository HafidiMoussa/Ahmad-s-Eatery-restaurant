Our Story

From Humble Beginnings

Ahmad's Middle Eastern Eatery began as a small family-run restaurant in 2010, founded by Ahmad Al-Farsi who immigrated from Lebanon with a dream of sharing his family's culinary traditions. What started as a modest 10-table eatery has now grown into one of the city's most beloved Middle Eastern restaurants.

Ahmad's passion for authentic flavors and warm hospitality quickly gained a loyal following. Today, while we've expanded our space and menu, we remain committed to the same principles that guided us from the beginning: quality ingredients, traditional recipes, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Our Culinary Philosophy

At the heart of our kitchen is the belief that food should tell a story. Each dish on our menu is crafted using recipes passed down through generations, with spices and techniques authentic to their regions of origin.
